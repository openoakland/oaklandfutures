National Day of Civic Hacking 2016

Join the Code for America Brigades, Secondmuse, and NASA for this year's National Day of Civic Hacking on June 4th, 2016. National Day of Civic Hacking is a nationwide day of action where developers, government employees, designers, journalists, data scientists, non-profit employees, UX designers, and residents who care about their communities come together to host civic tech events leveraging their skills to help their community.
